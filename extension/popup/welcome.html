SmartMate AI

Your AI-Powered Browser Sidekick

Summarize

Quickly summarize any selected text on a webpage to get the key points.

Rewrite

Rewrite selected text to improve clarity, tone, or style while maintaining the original meaning.

Expand

Expand brief notes or bullet points into fully-formed paragraphs with additional details.

Adjust Tone

Change the tone of selected text to match your desired style: formal, casual, professional, and more.

Getting Started

1
Select text on any webpage that you want to work with.
2
Click the SmartMate AI icon that appears near your selection, or use the right-click context menu.
3
Choose an action from the menu: Summarize, Rewrite, Expand, or Adjust Tone.
4
View the result in the popup modal and copy or replace the original text as needed.
